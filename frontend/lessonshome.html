Mathematics Lessons

Algebra

Learn the basics of algebra, including variables, equations, and functions. Understand how to solve linear equations and work with algebraic expressions.

Geometry

Dive into the world of shapes, sizes, and the properties of space. Explore different geometric figures and understand theorems related to angles, circles, and triangles.

Trigonometry

Understand the relationships between the sides and angles of triangles. Learn about sine, cosine, tangent, and their applications in real-world problems.

Calculus

Explore the fundamental concepts of calculus, including limits, derivatives, and integrals. Learn how calculus is used to solve complex problems in various fields.
